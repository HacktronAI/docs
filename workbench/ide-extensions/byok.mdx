Hacktron IDE extensions support **Bring Your Own Keys (BYOK)**, so you can connect your own LLM keys from the extension settings.

## Why use BYOK

- Use models and provider access managed by your team
- Keep your editor workflow aligned with your own LLM accounts
- Configure access without leaving the extension

## In the extension

Open the Hacktron settings in VS Code, Cursor, or Windsurf to configure your keys.

First, add the model provider you want to use from the extension settings.

<Card img="/images/add_model.png">
  Add a model provider directly from the Hacktron extension settings.
</Card>

Then review the model configuration and save the provider details.

<Card img="/images/model_config.png">
  Configure the provider and model settings without leaving your editor.
</Card>

Once configured, you can keep using the extension with the same Hacktron workflows inside your editor.
