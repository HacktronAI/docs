---
title: "Personal Agents"
description: "Create and manage personal Hacktron agents directly in the IDE extension"
---

You can create and manage **personal agents** directly in the IDE extension from the settings tab.

This lets you tailor Hacktron to your codebase and workflows without leaving your editor. Personal agents are private to you and can be updated as your workflows evolve.

<Card img="/images/extension-personal-agents.png">
  Create and manage personal agents directly in the extension settings.
</Card>

## Why use personal agents

- Capture patterns that are specific to your codebase
- Reuse custom workflows directly from the editor
- Keep private agent configuration tied to your own account
